## ADD

**PARAMETERS**

a (double or int) - The first number.
b (double or int) - The second number. 

**EXAMPLE USAGE**

```cpp
    int a = 10;
    double b = 10.5;

    cout << add(a, b) << endl;
```

Output: `20.5`
